Agriculture and Innovation

Nestled in the sunny, seaside city of Ventura, California, FreshRealm HQ is less than a mile from the beach and right in the heart of a thriving downtown hub. Ventura offers sunny skies, a warm climate, and a tech-centric entrepreneurial spirit. Known for its ideal surfing conditions and relaxed coastal vibe, Ventura was first established as the heart of a rich agricultural region carved out by the Santa Clara River making it the perfect spot for promoting well-being through fresh food and fresh thinking. FreshRealm actively encourages our team members to take advantage of everything our company and this warm, welcoming community have to offer.

FreshRealm also operates facilities across the United States with opportunities to join the team in Dallas, Indianapolis, Philadelphia, Riverside (CA), Sacramento and Seattle.
